## Success Conditions

### Setup

- Open with Mankey or have one on the Bench by Turn 2.
- Have at least one backup Mankey in play by Turn 3.

### Evolution

- Evolve into Primeape by Turn 2-3.
- Get your first Annihilape attacking by Turn 3-4.

### Damage Engine

- Have enough damage counters from Risky Ruins, Hawlucha, or similar setup to activate Lose Cool before your first Impact Blow.
- Attack with at least one full-power 280-damage Impact Blow during the game.

### Risky Ruins

- Get Risky Ruins into play by Turn 1-2.
- Place damage only where it helps, without accidentally giving up easy prizes.

### Hand Quality

- Never spend more than one turn with no productive play.
- Always have a path to your next attacker after one gets Knocked Out.

### Prize Race

- Take the first 2-Prize Knock Out, or stay even in the prize trade until your first Annihilape is online.
